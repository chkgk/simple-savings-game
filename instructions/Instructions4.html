Cash is the sum of:

  • 1) Your previous Cash balance
  • 2) The Interest Earned last month
  • 3) Your monthly Salary Income
  • 4) The Total Asset Payment from your investment in the previous month

Additionally, your Cash balance will decrease based on your Total Expenses, which consist of:

  • 5) Food Expenses
  • 6) Asset Investments
