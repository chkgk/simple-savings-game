To survive from one month to the next, you must consume 1 unit of Food each month.

There are two ways to meet this requirement:

  1. By purchasing Food: You buy at least 1 unit during the current month.
  2. From Food Reserves: You already have Food stored from earlier months.

If you neither have any Food in Reserves nor buy any Food during the month, you do not survive, and the round ends.

Each month, you have the option to buy Food at the current price listed in the "Information" section.

  • You will consume 1 unit of Food per month.
  • Food is nonperishable, so any units you buy in excess of 1 are stored as Food Reserves and carried over to future months.
  • If you already have Food in Reserves, you can choose not to buy any more.

You are not required to start a month with Food in your Reserves. As long as you buy at least 1 unit during the current month, you will survive to the next.

Examples:

  • If you have 3 units in Food Reserves, you can survive 3 months without buying anything.
  • If the month number + your Food Reserves = 13, you will survive until the end of the round.
